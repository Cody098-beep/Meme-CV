About

MemeCV is a tiny collection of meme-flavored resume templates designed to be funny but readable. Built to help you stand out—without getting ghosted by ATS. Here, you can craft the kind of resume that might not get you a 9-to-5, but could land you the role of “Chief Vibe Officer” at your friend’s garage startup. Why blend in when you can stand out like a cat wearing a banana costume.
